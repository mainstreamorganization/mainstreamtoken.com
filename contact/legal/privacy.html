1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how Mainstream Token ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, protects, and shares personal information on the mainstreamtoken.com website (the "Website") and through any associated services or interactions with Mainstream Token ($MAIN) and the Mainstream Token License protocol (MTL Protocol). This policy should be read in conjunction with our Legal Documentation and Disclaimer and Terms of Use, both of which are incorporated by reference. By using our Website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we acknowledge the importance of complying with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

If you are located in a jurisdiction that imposes stricter requirements (e.g., the European Union, California), we will comply with those requirements, including obtaining your explicit consent, where necessary.

2. DEFINITIONS

  • Personal Information: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (“data subject”), which may include, but is not limited to, name, email address, IP address, device information, and usage patterns.
  • Website: Refers to the mainstreamtoken.com website and all associated platforms and pages.
  • $MAIN: Refers to the Mainstream Token, a decentralized cryptocurrency token.
  • MTL Protocol: Refers to the Mainstream Token License protocol, a decentralized system for managing digital rights.
  • User/You/Your: Refers to any individual or entity accessing or using the Website, $MAIN, or the MTL Protocol.
  • Cookie Policy: Refers to the cookie policy, found at /contact/legal/cookies.
  • Terms of Use: Refers to the Terms of Use document, found at /contact/legal/terms.
  • Legal Disclaimer: Refers to the Legal Disclaimer, found at /contact/legal/disclaimer.

3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Information You Provide Directly: When you voluntarily provide information through the Website, such as when you contact us through a contact form, sign up for a newsletter or register for an account. This information may include your name, email address, or any other information you choose to provide.
  • Automatically Collected Data: As you use the Website, certain data is automatically collected:
    • Technical Data: This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and screen resolution.
    • Usage Data: This includes your interactions with the website, including pages visited, links clicked, time spent on the Website, and other browsing activities.
    • Cookie Data: The Website uses a "session" cookie to facilitate secure access to the site, and to facilitate user session management. This cookie is essential to the functioning of the website, and will be removed automatically 24 hours after you log in, or after you log out. For more details about our cookie usage, including cookie lifespan and necessity, please refer to our [Cookie Policy]([Cookie Policy Link]).
  • Information from Third Parties: We do not currently collect any information about you from third parties. Should we begin to do so, we will update this Privacy Policy.
  • Transaction Data: In the future, if you purchase, sell, or license any digital assets on our site using $MAIN tokens, we may collect data about these transactions. This may include information about your crypto wallet address, the amount of tokens transferred, the transaction history, and other information associated with those transactions.

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To Operate and Improve the Website: To maintain and improve the functionality and user experience of the Website.
  • User Support: To address your inquiries, and to provide customer service, and technical support.
  • Security: To protect the Website, our systems, and users from fraud and abuse.
  • Communications: To send you updates, newsletters, and other relevant communications (when you opt in to these communications).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations or regulatory requirements.
  • Transaction Verification: To properly process and verify any digital asset transactions on the website.

5. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal data based on our legitimate interests in maintaining and improving our Website, fulfilling contractual obligations when you use our services, and compliance with legal requirements where applicable. In certain instances, such as the setting of non-essential cookies, we rely on your explicit consent.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal data collected only for as long as is necessary for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. When no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymize your data using secure digital erasure or anonymization procedures.

  • Data that is voluntarily provided by the user, will be retained until the user requests it is deleted, or that the data is no longer needed.
  • Data collected automatically will be retained for one year, after which it will be anonymized or deleted.
  • Transaction data will be retained indefinitely, but will be anonymized as soon as reasonably possible.
  • Session cookies will be retained for 24 hours from login.

We retain user contact data only for as long as is necessary to respond to your inquiry, or to provide account services.

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including the use of secure data storage, data encryption, and physical controls at our facilities. However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of your account credentials.

8. SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell, lease, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your personal data only as necessary:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us with website hosting, customer service, technical support, payment processing (if applicable), and security services, or other third-party services that require data access to fulfill their function for you. These third parties are obligated to protect your data in compliance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, legal process, or regulatory request.
  • Business Transfers: We may share your information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets.
  • With your Consent: We may share your data with other third-parties with your explicit consent.

We do not sell or share personal information in exchange for monetary or other valuable consideration. If this practice changes in the future, this document will be updated and we will seek your consent for such data sharing if applicable.

9. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: The right to have inaccurate or incomplete personal data corrected.
  • Right to Erasure: The right to have your personal data erased (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Right to Restriction: The right to restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: The right to receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: The right to object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: The right to withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, if you believe that your data is being mishandled.

You may submit a request to exercise your rights by emailing us at [Insert Contact Email]. We may require proof of identity to verify and process your request. We will respond to your requests within 30 days, in compliance with GDPR or other relevant regulations.

10. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

By using our Website, you consent to the processing of your personal data in countries that may have data protection laws different than those in your jurisdiction, as our servers are located outside of the European Union. We employ Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other recognized mechanisms for cross-border transfers, and we will take reasonable measures to protect your personal data as outlined in this policy, and in compliance with all applicable laws.

11. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies, primarily for the purpose of authentication and session management. Please refer to our [Cookie Policy]([Cookie Policy Link]) for a more detailed description about what cookies we use, and their purpose. Session cookies are retained for 24 hours from login, or until the user logs out.

12. THIRD PARTY LINKS

Our website may contain links to other websites. Please note that any links to a third-party website does not mean that we endorse that site, or that we are responsible for its privacy or data collection policies. You agree that we are not responsible for, nor liable for, any data that is collected by those third-party sites. You must review each individual site's privacy policy before proceeding to use it.

13. CONSENT TO DATA COLLECTION

By using our website, you are agreeing to the collection, storage, and processing of your personal data as specified in this policy, and you understand your rights under this policy.

14. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided personal information without parental consent, we will promptly delete it.

15. AUTOMATED DECISION MAKING

We do not currently engage in any automated decision-making processes using your personal data.

16. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will post any changes on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will indicate when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of the Website after such posting constitutes Your acceptance of the updated policy. It is your responsibility to check the Privacy Policy for any changes. If we begin collecting data from new sources or significantly change how we process personal information, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you via the Website or by email (where required).

17. LEGAL DISCLAIMER

This Privacy Policy is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. We recommend you consult with a qualified legal professional to ensure your full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. This Privacy Policy is also subject to the limitations and disclaimers set forth in our Legal Documentation and Disclaimer. In the event of a conflict between the Privacy Policy and the Legal Documentation, the Legal Documentation will govern.

18. CONTACT US

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us at: https://github.com/mainstreamorganization/mainstreamtoken/discussions
